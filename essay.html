Secondary School and College Essay Guidance

Customized Services Based on Individual Needs

Effective Secondary School and College essays are authentic, memorable and personal. They tell a compelling narrative and capture the attention of the admission officer. Some essays only require proofreading, and others require guidance from ideation through the final draft. We help with both, and everything in between. We are accustomed to tutoring in person and over the phone and to reviewing drafts by email. So we can work with local, national or international students.

Our Approach Includes:

  • Detailed written responses and phone/video chat time to brainstorm and draw out compelling narratives
  • Candid and specific feedback on drafts
  • Email synopsis following every consultation
  • Motivation and deadline timetables

The Cost

Services start at $150 an hour. Package rates can be negotiated for Full Essays, personal statements and supplemental essays
